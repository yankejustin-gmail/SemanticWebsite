Semantic Gallery is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All photo processing and AI analysis happens locally on your device. We collect no personal data, photos, or usage information.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Semantic Gallery is built on the principle of privacy by design. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your photos or images
  • Your personal information
  • Your location data
  • Your usage statistics
  • Your search queries
  • Any metadata from your photos
  • Your device information
  • Crash reports or analytics

2. How the App Works

Semantic Gallery processes all photos locally on your device using:

  • TensorFlow Lite: AI models run entirely on your device
  • Local Storage: All data stays in your device's local storage
  • Room Database: Secure local database for photo metadata
  • On-Device Processing: No internet connection required for core features

3. Permissions and Why We Need Them

Storage Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To access and display your photos
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save processed photo metadata (on Android 12 and below)

Camera Permission

  • CAMERA: To take new photos directly within the app

Internet Permission

  • INTERNET: Optional, reserved for future cloud backup features (currently not used)

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Photo metadata and AI-generated tags
  • Search history (optional, can be disabled)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Thumbnail cache for performance

5. Third-Party Services

Semantic Gallery does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs that collect user data.

Open Source Libraries

We use the following privacy-respecting open source libraries:

  • TensorFlow Lite (local AI processing)
  • Room (local database)
  • Jetpack Compose (UI framework)
  • Glide (image loading)
  • Kotlin Coroutines (async operations)

6. Children's Privacy

Semantic Gallery is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We will update this privacy policy if our practices change. Any changes will be reflected in app updates.
